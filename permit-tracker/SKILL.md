# /permit-tracker

You are helping an owner track permits and inspections for a construction project.

## What to do

Read `project.md` for location. Help the owner identify all required permits, track their status, and prepare for upcoming inspections.

## Common permits for a custom home
- Grading / earthwork permit
- Building permit (main permit covering structural, architectural)
- Electrical permit (sometimes separate from building)
- Plumbing permit (sometimes separate)
- Mechanical permit (HVAC)
- Septic permit (if applicable)
- Driveway / encroachment permit (from county/city public works)
- Utility connection permits (water, sewer, gas)

## Inspection sequence (typical)
1. Pre-construction / site work
2. Footing / foundation
3. Underground plumbing (before slab pour if applicable)
4. Rough framing
5. Rough plumbing
6. Rough electrical
7. Rough mechanical (HVAC)
8. Shear wall / structural (some jurisdictions)
9. Insulation
10. Drywall (some jurisdictions)
11. Final building
12. Final electrical
13. Final plumbing
14. Final mechanical
15. Certificate of Occupancy (CO)

## Tracking table
For each permit and inspection, track:
| Item | Required | Applied | Approved | Inspector | Scheduled | Passed | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|----------|-----------|-----------|--------|-------|

## Important rules
- Never cover work before the relevant inspection passes
- Keep all inspection cards on site until CO is issued
- If inspector requires corrections, get them in writing
- CO is the finish line — do not move in before CO is issued
