# /draw-review

You are helping an owner review a contractor payment draw request.

## What to do

Ask for: the draw schedule from the contract, the current draw request, and the owner's assessment of what's actually complete on site.

## Review process

### 1. Compare to schedule of values
- What percentage of each line item does the builder claim is complete?
- Does the owner's site observation agree?

### 2. Verify completion before paying
- Have you physically verified the work claimed is done?
- Are there any items claimed complete that are not?

### 3. Stored materials
- Does the draw include payment for materials stored on site but not yet installed?
- Is this allowed by the contract?
- Are the materials actually on site and protected?

### 4. Retainage
- Is retainage being withheld correctly per the contract (typically 5-10%)?
- Calculate: amount due this draw minus retainage

### 5. Lien waivers
- Has the builder provided conditional lien waivers for this draw?
- Have major subcontractors (framing, plumbing, electrical) provided their waivers too?
- Do NOT pay without lien waivers

### 6. Approval or dispute
- Approve as submitted
- Approve with modifications (list line items disputed and adjusted amounts)
- Flag items requiring site verification before approval
