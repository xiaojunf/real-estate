# /punch-list

You are helping an owner generate and track a construction punch list at project completion.

## What to do

Walk the owner through a systematic room-by-room and system-by-system review to generate a complete punch list. Track items to completion.

## Punch list by category

### Site / exterior
- Final grading drains away from foundation
- Driveway and walkways complete and sealed
- Landscaping complete per contract
- Exterior paint complete, no missed spots
- All exterior lights functional
- Gutters and downspouts installed and draining correctly

### Structure / envelope
- No visible cracks in foundation
- Roof complete, no missing shingles
- All windows and doors operate smoothly
- Weatherstripping on all exterior doors
- No air gaps around windows (visible from interior)

### For each room
- Walls: paint complete, no patches visible, no scuffs
- Ceiling: paint complete, texture consistent
- Floor: no chips, scratches, hollow spots
- Trim: painted, caulked, no gaps at joints
- Doors: operate smoothly, latch correctly, stops installed
- Windows: operate correctly, screens present
- Outlets and switches: cover plates on, all functional
- Lights: all functional, trim rings flush

### Kitchen
- Cabinets: all doors and drawers operate, hardware complete
- Countertops: no chips, caulked at backsplash
- Sink and faucet functional, no leaks
- Appliances operational and clean
- Hood vented correctly

### Bathrooms (each)
- Tile grouted and sealed
- No caulk missing at tub/shower
- Fixtures functional, no leaks
- Exhaust fan operational

### Mechanical systems
- HVAC: all zones heat and cool, registers functional
- Water heater operational
- All plumbing fixtures run without leaks
- Electrical panel labeled correctly

## Tracking
For each item: description, location, trade responsible, target completion date, completed date.

Do not release final retainage until all punch list items are complete and verified.
