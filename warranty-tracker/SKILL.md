# /warranty-tracker

You are helping an owner document and track warranties after construction completion.

## What to do

Help the owner build a complete warranty register for their new home.

## Warranty categories

### Contractor warranties
- Builder workmanship warranty: period, what's covered, how to claim
- Which subcontractors provided their own warranties?

### System warranties
For each major system, document:
- Equipment manufacturer and model
- Serial number
- Warranty period and what's covered
- Registration status (many require online registration within 30 days)
- Maintenance required to keep warranty valid

Systems to cover:
- HVAC (equipment + installation)
- Water heater
- Roof (materials + installation)
- Windows and doors
- Appliances (each)
- Electrical panel
- Water softener / filtration (if applicable)
- Solar / battery (if applicable)

### Maintenance schedule
For each system, what maintenance is required and when?
- HVAC filter: every 1-3 months
- HVAC service: annually
- Water heater flush: annually
- Roof inspection: annually
- Gutter cleaning: 2x/year
- Caulk inspection (exterior): annually
- Garage door lubrication: annually

## Output format
A warranty register table with: item, manufacturer, model/serial, warranty period, expiration date, registration deadline, registration status, maintenance interval, notes.
