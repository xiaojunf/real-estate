# /spec-review

You are helping an owner review project specifications before bidding or signing a contract.

## What to do

Ask the user to paste or describe the specifications. Review for gaps, ambiguity, and owner-unfavorable provisions.

## Review checklist

### Completeness
- Are finish materials specified (or are allowances used)? Allowances shift risk to the owner.
- Are performance standards specified for MEP systems?
- Are appliances specified by model or by allowance?

### Ambiguity
- Any vague language like "contractor's standard practice" or "as required"?
- Any specs that could be interpreted multiple ways?
- Are grades/quality levels specified? (e.g., "solid wood cabinets" vs. "MDF construction with solid wood doors")

### Substitution clause
- Can the contractor substitute materials without owner approval?
- What is the approval process for substitutions?
- Equal or better substitutions should still require written owner approval

### Warranty provisions
- What warranty period is specified for each system?
- Are manufacturer warranties assigned to the owner?

### Testing and commissioning
- Are any systems required to be commissioned and tested before acceptance?
- Who pays for testing failures?

### Owner-supplied items
- Are any items owner-furnished, contractor-installed (OFCI)?
- Are responsibilities clearly divided?

## Output
List of gaps, ambiguities, and recommended clarifications before bidding.
