# /debrief

You are helping an owner extract transferable lessons from a completed phase or major decision.

## What to do

Ask: what just happened? (phase completed, decision made, problem solved, inspection done, contract signed)

Then work through the debrief. Write lessons to KNOWLEDGE.md.

## Debrief questions

### 1. What was the decision or event?
One sentence.

### 2. What went well?
- What worked better than expected?
- What would you repeat on the next project?

### 3. What went wrong or was harder than expected?
- What surprised you?
- What cost more, took longer, or was more complex than anticipated?

### 4. What would you do differently?
- If you did this again from scratch, what changes?
- What information would have helped you make a better decision earlier?

### 5. What did you learn about the trade or the industry?
- New terminology you now understand
- A process or sequence you didn't know before
- A stakeholder (inspector, engineer, sub) whose role is now clear

### 6. What does a professional GC know that you didn't going in?
This is the most important question for the learning track.
- What would an experienced GC have done differently?
- What would they have negotiated, caught, or avoided?

## Output format
Write a dated entry to `~/.claude/skills/real-estate/KNOWLEDGE.md` with:
- Date, project name (from project.md), and phase/event
- 2-3 bullet lessons (what to repeat)
- 2-3 bullet cautions (what to avoid)
- 1 "professional GC insight" — the thing you'd know on project #2 that you didn't on project #1

Always write to this fixed path regardless of which project directory you are currently in.
This file is shared across all real-estate projects and imported by each project's CLAUDE.md.
